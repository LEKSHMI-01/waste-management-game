Welcome to the Waste Management Game! Let's learn about different types of waste.
Waste can be divided into two main categories: recyclable and non-recyclable.
Recyclable waste includes items like paper, cardboard, glass, and certain types of plastic.
Non-recyclable waste includes items like food waste, used tissues, and certain types of plastic that cannot be recycled.
In the game, your task will be to sort waste items into these two categories. Are you ready to play?
