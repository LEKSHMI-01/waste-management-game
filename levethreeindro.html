' EcoSort '
Welcome to the LEVEL 3! Follow these steps to learn how to play.
The goal of the game is to correctly sort different types of waste into their respective piles within the given time limit.
There are three sorting piles: PILE, PLASTIC PILE, and HAZARDOUS PILE. Each pile is designated for a specific type of waste: biofuel, plastic, or hazardous waste. To sort an item, drag and drop it into the appropriate pile based on its type
Your score starts at 0 points. Correctly sorting an item into its respective pile earns you 10 points. Incorrectly sorting an item deducts points based on the number of times it has been wrongly sorted. Your total score is displayed at the top right corner of the screen.
You have 1 minute (60 seconds) to complete the sorting task. The timer begins as soon as you start playing the game. Complete the sorting within the time limit to win the game.
Successfully sort all the waste items into their respective piles and accumulate a total of 120 points to win the game. If the time runs out before completing the sorting, the game ends, and you can try again.
Enjoy the game and have fun testing your sorting skills!
